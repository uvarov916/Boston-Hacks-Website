What is Boston Hacks?

BostonHacks is Boston University's top hackathon. For 24 hours on October 31 to November 1, 500 students will come to Boston University, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively code a unique solution from scratch. Imagine and create the most impressive hacks during the weekend!

Who 500 students
When October 31 - November 1
Where Metcalf Hall, Boston University, MA
How long 24 hours of pure hacking

FAQ

Where is it?

George Sherman Union, 2nd Floor in Metcalf Hall at 775 Commonwealth Avenue, Boston, MA, 02215.

Who's coming?

We expect 500 hackers from all around the world to attend BostonHacks.

Who can attend?

All students are welcome. If you're under 18, we will need you to bring a signed parental consent form.

Do I need experience?

No experience necessary. Don't worry about being new to hackathons. We will have plenty of mentors and resources. Come, learn, and experience your first hackathon at BostonHacks!

Are there any rules?

We want to ensure a positive experience for every participant. We encourage you to read our Code of Conduct.

What should I bring?

You should bring your laptop, phone, chargers, and sleeping bag. All food and other accommodations will be taken care of.

Can we form teams?

Of course you can! We encourage people to work in teams of up to 4 people. You can work alone, but it won't be the same. There will be plenty of team building events on site and online.

Does it cost anything?

BostonHacks is 100% free. You won't have to spend a dime! Everything will be absolutely free.

Will there be travel reimbursements?

We're working on providing travel reimbursements, and we will provide the form later.
