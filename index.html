FAQ

Where is it?

George Sherman Union, 2nd Floor in Metcalf Hall at 775 Commonwealth Avenue, Boston, MA, 02215.

Who's coming?

We expect 500 hackers from all around the United States to attend Boston Hacks.

Who can attend?

All students are welcome. If you're under 18, we will need you to bring a signed parental consent form.

Do I need experience?

No experience necessary. Don't worry about being new to hackathons. We will have plenty of mentors and resources. Come, learn, and experience your first hackathon at Boston Hacks!

What do I make?

Make anything you want! Software, hardware, whatever comes into your mind!

What should I bring?

You should bring your laptop, phone, chargers, and sleeping bag. All food and other accommodations will be taken care of.

Can we form teams?

Of course you can! We encourage people to work in teams of up to 4 people. You can work alone, but it won't be the same. There will be plenty of team building events on site and online.

Does it cost anything?

Boston Hacks is 100% free. You won't have to spend a dime! Everything will be absolutely free.

Will there be travel reimbursements?

We're working on providing travel reimbursements, and we will provide the form later.
